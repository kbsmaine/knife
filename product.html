FIELD NOTES

The Graveborn Recon is what happens when you mash a pry bar into a night-knife. Neutral balance, a tip that doesn't cry when it hits metal, and geometry that still slices cord and tape without feeling like a cleaver.

Spine and choil are softened for gloved work. The handle scales run a deep coffin shape with high traction carving into the corners so the knife stays put when everything else is slick.
